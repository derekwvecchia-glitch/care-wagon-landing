C.A.R.E. Wagon

About Us

Who · What · When · Where · Why

👥 Who

The C.A.R.E. Wagon is supported by members of the Revere Police Department and made possible through the support and generosity of City of Revere officials, local businesses, community organizations, and, most importantly, you—the community we proudly serve. Together, these partnerships help bring resources, engagement, and positive interactions to residents of all ages throughout the City of Revere.

🚐 What

C.A.R.E. stands for Community Access, Resources & Engagement. The C.A.R.E. Wagon is a mobile community outreach vehicle designed to bring positive interactions, information, and resources directly to the neighborhoods we serve. Whether providing a cup of coffee, an ice cream, a bicycle helmet, safety information, or connecting residents with available services, the C.A.R.E. Wagon helps strengthen relationships between the community and the Revere Police Department.

📅 When

The C.A.R.E. Wagon appears throughout the year at community events, schools, senior centers, parks, resource fairs, neighborhood gatherings, and special programs. Follow our event calendar and social media pages to find out where we will be next.

📍 Where

The C.A.R.E. Wagon serves communities throughout the City of Revere and participates in events across neighborhoods, schools, public spaces, and community organizations. Our goal is to bring outreach and engagement directly to the people we serve.

❤️ Why

The C.A.R.E. Wagon was created to foster trust, strengthen community relationships, and provide accessible support and resources in a welcoming environment. We believe that positive interactions and meaningful connections help build safer, stronger, and more resilient neighborhoods. By meeting people where they are, we strive to make resources more accessible and create opportunities for conversation, collaboration, and community engagement.

Serving Revere with C.A.R.E.

One neighborhood, one conversation, and one connection at a time.

The C.A.R.E. Wagon reflects the Revere Police Department's commitment to community policing and its belief that strong relationships are built through compassion, accessibility, and meaningful engagement. Whether sharing a cup of coffee, handing out ice cream, providing safety information, or connecting residents with valuable resources, the C.A.R.E. Wagon is dedicated to Serving Revere with C.A.R.E.
